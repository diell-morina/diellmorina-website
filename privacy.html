☀️ Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how I, Diell Morina, handle data on this portfolio website. I value your privacy and aim to be completely transparent about how visitor interactions are measured.

Why I Ask for Your Consent: As someone working at the intersection of data and marketing engineering, I rely on accurate analytics to understand how this website performs in the real world. When you click Accept, you allow me to see anonymous metrics like which pages are visited most often, what device types are being used, and visual heatmaps that show where layout elements might be breaking or causing friction. This data is used strictly for site optimization and measuring project effectiveness. I do not collect personally identifiable information through these scripts, and your data is never sold or transferred to third-party data brokers.

2. Analytics & User Behavior Tracking

I use industry-standard tools to analyze traffic and optimize website usability:

  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4): Helps me understand page views, session durations, and general interaction metrics.
  • Microsoft Clarity: Helps me record anonymous user sessions, analyze clicks, and create visual heatmaps to improve my layout.

No Hidden Trackers or Beacons: Aside from the two analytics tools explicitly listed above, there are zero unlisted third-party trackers, web beacons, tracking pixels, or device fingerprinting scripts embedded in this website. What is documented on this page is the exact and complete inventory of what runs under the hood.

3. Cookies & Storage Inventory

I use cookies (small text files placed on your browser) and local storage to run my analytics tools and remember your preferences. Here is the exact inventory of what I use:

Tool Cookie Name Duration Purpose
Google Analytics _ga 2 years Assigns an anonymous, randomly generated ID to distinguish unique users.
Google Analytics _ga_NZPK20MGDJ 2 years Tracks your current active session state and session duration.
Microsoft Clarity _clck 1 year Stores an anonymous ID for Clarity to recognize returning visitors.

This Website cookieConsent Permanent A Local Storage variable that remembers if you clicked "Accept" on my banner.

4. Data I Collect & How It Is Protected

Tracking is OFF by default: When you visit this website, Google Tag Manager and all analytics scripts are completely blocked from loading. Zero cookies are stored and zero network requests are sent to analytics providers unless you explicitly click "Accept" on the cookie banner. Ignoring or declining the banner guarantees 100% anonymous, untracked browsing. There are no hidden web beacons, third-party advertising scripts, or unacknowledged tracking pixels anywhere on this website.

Personal Data (Contact Form): If you fill out the contact form, your First Name, Last Name, Email Address, and Message are securely transmitted via Web3Forms directly to my personal email inbox. This data is used exclusively to reply to your inquiry and is never shared, rented, or sold.

5. Your Rights, Withdrawal & Data Deletion

You have total control over your data at all times:

  • Withdraw Cookie Consent: If you previously accepted cookies, you can revoke consent immediately by clicking Reset Cookie Consent. This wipes your local storage and halts all tracking.
  • Delete Contact Submissions: Since contact form submissions are forwarded via Web3Forms and stored directly in my personal email inbox, you have the full right to be forgotten. Simply reply to our email thread or send a new message via the contact form requesting deletion. I will permanently delete your message history from my inbox, and Web3Forms automatically purges all delivery logs from their servers.
