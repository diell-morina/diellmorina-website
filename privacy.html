Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how I, Diell Morina, handle data on this portfolio website. I value your privacy and aim to be completely transparent about how visitor interactions are measured.

2. Analytics & User Behavior Tracking

I use industry-standard tools to analyze traffic and optimize website usability:

  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4): Helps me understand page views, session durations, and user interactions.
  • Microsoft Clarity: Helps me record user sessions, analyze mouse movements, clicks, and create visual heatmaps to improve my site layouts.

3. Cookies & Storage Inventory

I use cookies (small text files placed on your browser) and local storage to run my analytics tools and remember your preferences. Here is the exact inventory of what I use:

Tool Cookie Name Duration Purpose
Google Analytics _ga 2 years Assigns an anonymous, randomly generated ID to distinguish unique users.
Google Analytics _ga_NZPK20MGDJ 2 years Tracks your current active session state and session duration.
Microsoft Clarity _clck 1 year Stores an anonymous ID for Clarity to recognize returning visitors.
Microsoft Clarity _clsk 30 mins Connects multiple page views by a single user into a single session recording.
This Website cookieConsent Permanent A Local Storage variable that remembers if you clicked "Accept" on my banner.

4. Data I Collect

Anonymized Data (Analytics): I collect technical and behavioral information (device type, operating system, browser, general location/city level, scroll depth, mouse clicks, and session recordings). No Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is linked to this data.

Personal Data (Contact Form): If you fill out the contact form, your First Name, Last Name, Email, and Message are securely sent via Web3Forms to my inbox. This data is purely used to reply to your inquiry and is never shared, rented, or sold by me.

5. Your Rights & Control

You can block cookies entirely via your web browser settings, or use privacy shields (like Brave, Firefox tracking protection, or adblockers). The site will remain fully accessible even if you opt out of cookie tracking.
