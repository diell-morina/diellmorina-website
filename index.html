About Me

I live in Kosovo, and I approach digital marketing by balancing creative strategy with structured data. I like thinking creatively about campaigns, but what I really love is digging into messy numbers, structuring them, and finding the patterns that actually drive revenue. Since March 2024, I've been working as a Media Buyer at Origin3 Agency, managing ad campaigns end-to-end for SMEs. I handle everything from strategy and account management to scaling budgets and performance reporting. Along the way, I've deployed millions in ad spend, generated millions in client revenue, and completely rebuilt our agency's internal workflows to make operations run much sharper.

I already do a lot of marketing tech work, especially building custom tracking architectures, attribution models, and reporting dashboards. But I want to go further into the analytics side. Right now, I'm focusing on advanced data analysis and actively learning R, Python, and JavaScript. My goal is to move beyond standard ad platforms, write my own code, and build custom tools that make campaign performance more predictable.
